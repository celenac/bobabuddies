ABOUT THE GAME

The Boba Buddies are on a mission to find the best boba shop in the city. It's up to you to get them there safely! Boba Buddies is a game created during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in 2015. Girls Who Code is a national non-profit organization working to close the gender gap in the technology and engineering sectors.

PLAYING ON PC

Press the number keys on the keyboard.

PLAYING ON MOBILE

Hold your mobile device horizontally.

Music might not work on mobile.
